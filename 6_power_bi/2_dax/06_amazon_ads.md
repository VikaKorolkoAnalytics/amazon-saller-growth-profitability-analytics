# 06 Amazon Ads Measures
This folder contains measures used to evaluate Amazon Advertising performance, traffic generation, and advertising cost efficiency.

The measures support campaign analysis, marketing optimization, and advertising performance monitoring.

---
## Amazon Impressions
```DAX
Amazon Impressions =
SUM(fact_amazon_ads[impressions])
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the total number of Amazon ad impressions.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures advertising reach and campaign visibility.

---
## Amazon Clicks
```DAX
Amazon Clicks =
SUM(fact_amazon_ads[clicks])
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the total number of clicks generated by Amazon Ads.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures customer engagement with Amazon advertising campaigns.

---
## Amazon Spend
```DAX
Amazon Spend =
SUM(fact_amazon_ads[spend])
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates total advertising spend across Amazon Ads campaigns.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures investment in Amazon advertising activities.

---
## Amazon CTR (Click-Through Rate)
```DAX
Amazon CTR (Click-Through Rate) =
DIVIDE(
	[Amazon Clicks],
	[Amazon Impressions]
)
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures the effectiveness of Amazon advertising creatives and targeting.

---
## Amazon CPC (Cost Per Click)
```DAX
Amazon CPC (Cost Per Click) =
DIVIDE(
	[Amazon Spend],
	[Amazon Clicks]
)
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the average advertising cost per click.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures traffic acquisition efficiency and helps monitor advertising costs.

---
## Business Value
These measures help evaluate:
- Amazon advertising reach and visibility.
- Campaign engagement performance.
- Advertising traffic generation.
- Cost efficiency of Amazon Ads campaigns.
- Marketing budget optimization.
- Campaign performance monitoring.
- Advertising investment effectiveness.
