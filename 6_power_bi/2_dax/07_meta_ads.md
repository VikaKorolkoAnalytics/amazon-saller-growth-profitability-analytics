# 07 Meta Ads Measures
This folder contains measures used to evaluate Meta Ads performance, audience engagement, and advertising cost efficiency.

The measures support campaign analysis, traffic generation monitoring, and marketing optimization.

---
## Meta Impressions
```DAX
Meta Impressions =
SUM(fact_meta_ads[impressions])
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the total number of Meta Ads impressions.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures advertising reach and audience exposure across Meta platforms.

---
## Meta Clicks
```DAX
Meta Clicks =
SUM(fact_meta_ads[clicks])
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the total number of clicks generated by Meta Ads campaigns.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures audience engagement and traffic generated through Meta advertising.

---
## Meta Spend
```DAX
Meta Spend =
SUM(fact_meta_ads[spend])
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates total advertising spend across Meta Ads campaigns.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures investment in Meta advertising activities.

---
## Meta CTR (Click-Through Rate)
```DAX
Meta CTR (Click-Through Rate) =
DIVIDE(
	[Meta Clicks],
	[Meta Impressions]
)
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures the effectiveness of Meta advertising creatives and audience targeting.

---
## Meta CPC (Cost Per Click)
```DAX
Meta CPC (Cost Per Click) =
DIVIDE(
	[Meta Spend],
	[Meta Clicks]
)
```
**Purpose:** 
Calculates the average advertising cost per click.

**Business Meaning:** 
Measures traffic acquisition efficiency and helps evaluate advertising cost effectiveness.

---
## Business Value
These measures help evaluate:
- Meta advertising reach and audience exposure.
- Campaign engagement performance.
- Advertising traffic generation.
- Cost efficiency of Meta Ads campaigns.
- Marketing budget allocation.
- Campaign effectiveness and optimization opportunities.
- Cross-channel marketing performance comparisons.
