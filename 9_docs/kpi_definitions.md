
# KPI Definitions
This document defines the key business metrics used throughout the Amazon Seller Growth & Profitability Analytics solution.

---
## Revenue (Gross)
### Definition
```text
Revenue (Gross) = Total sales before refunds
```
### Business Meaning
Represents the total value of customer purchases before returns, refunds, and other deductions.

---
## Net Sales
### Definition
```text
Net Sales = Gross Revenue - Refund Amount
```
### Business Meaning
Represents revenue retained after customer refunds.

---
## Gross Profit
### Definition
```text
Gross Profit = Net Sales - COGS
```
### Business Meaning
Measures profitability after deducting product costs from Net Sales.

---
## Operating Profit (EBIT)
### Definition
```text
Operating Profit (EBIT) = Gross Profit - Operating Expenses
```
### Business Meaning
Measures profitability after deducting all operating costs, including marketplace fees and advertising expenses.

---
## COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
### Definition
```text
COGS = Quantity Sold × Cost Per Unit
```
### Business Meaning
Represents the direct cost of products sold during the reporting period.

---
## Gross Margin %
### Definition
```text
Gross Margin % = Gross Profit ÷ Net Sales
```
### Business Meaning
Measures product-level profitability and indicates how much revenue remains after product costs.

---
## Operating Margin %
### Definition
```text
Operating Margin % = Operating Profit ÷ Net Sales
```
### Business Meaning
Measures overall business profitability after all operating expenses have been deducted.

---
## Profit Per Unit
### Definition
```text
Profit Per Unit = Gross Profit ÷ Units Sold
```
### Business Meaning
Measures average profit generated from each unit sold.

---
## Total Orders
### Definition
```text
Total Orders = Distinct Count of Order IDs
```
### Business Meaning
Represents the total number of unique customer orders.

---
## Total Units Sold
### Definition
```text
Total Units Sold = Sum of Quantity Sold
```
### Business Meaning
Represents the total quantity of products sold.

---
## Average Order Value (AOV)
### Definition
```text
AOV = Gross Revenue ÷ Total Orders
```
### Business Meaning
Measures the average revenue generated per customer order.

---
## Unit Price
### Definition
```text
Unit Price = Gross Revenue ÷ Units Sold
```
### Business Meaning
Measures the average selling price per unit.

---
## Total Returned Units
### Definition
```text
Total Returned Units = Sum of Returned Quantity
```
### Business Meaning
Measures the total quantity of products returned by customers.

---
## Total Refund Amount
### Definition
```text
Total Refund Amount = Sum of Refund Amount
```
### Business Meaning
Measures the total monetary value refunded to customers.

---
## Return Rate %
### Definition
```text
Return Rate % = Returned Units ÷ Units Sold
```
### Business Meaning
Measures the percentage of sold units that were returned.

---
## Total Refund Fees
### Definition
```text
Total Refund Fees = Sum of Refund Fee Reimbursements
```
### Business Meaning
Represents Amazon refund fee reimbursements that partially offset return-related losses.

---
## Total Ad Spend (Combined)
### Definition
```text
Total Ad Spend =
Amazon Ads Spend + Meta Ads Spend
```
### Business Meaning
Measures total advertising investment across all marketing channels.

---
## Amazon Spend
### Definition
```text
Amazon Spend = Sum of Amazon Ads Spend
```
### Business Meaning
Measures advertising spend allocated to Amazon Ads campaigns.

---
## Meta Spend
### Definition
```text
Meta Spend = Sum of Meta Ads Spend
```
### Business Meaning
Measures advertising spend allocated to Meta Ads campaigns.

---
## Total Impressions
### Definition
```text
Total Impressions =
Amazon Impressions + Meta Impressions
```
### Business Meaning
Measures total advertising exposure across all channels.

---
## Total Clicks
### Definition
```text
Total Clicks =
Amazon Clicks + Meta Clicks
```
### Business Meaning
Measures customer engagement generated through advertising campaigns.

---
## CTR (Click-Through Rate)
### Definition
```text
CTR = Clicks ÷ Impressions
```
### Business Meaning
Measures the percentage of impressions that resulted in clicks.

---
## CPC (Cost Per Click)
### Definition
```text
CPC = Ad Spend ÷ Clicks
```
### Business Meaning
Measures average advertising cost per click.

---
## TACoS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales)
### Definition
```text
TACoS = Total Ad Spend ÷ Net Sales
```
### Business Meaning
Measures advertising spend as a percentage of Net Sales and evaluates overall marketing efficiency.

---
## Color Gross Revenue Contribution %
### Definition
```text
Color Revenue Contribution % =
Product Color Revenue ÷ Total Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures how much each product color contributes to total revenue.

---
## Color Gross Profit Contribution %
### Definition
```text
Color Profit Contribution % =
Product Color Gross Profit ÷ Total Gross Profit
```
### Business Meaning
Measures how much each product color contributes to total profitability.

---
## Gross Revenue MTD
### Definition
```text
Month-to-Date Gross Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures accumulated revenue from the beginning of the current month through the selected date.

---
## Gross Revenue YTD
### Definition
```text
Year-to-Date Gross Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures accumulated revenue from the beginning of the year through the selected date.

---
## Gross Revenue MoM %
### Definition
```text
(Current Month Revenue − Previous Month Revenue)
÷ Previous Month Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures month-over-month revenue growth.

---
## Net Sales MoM %
### Definition
```text
(Current Month Net Sales − Previous Month Net Sales)
÷ Previous Month Net Sales
```
### Business Meaning
Measures month-over-month growth in Net Sales.

---
## Operating Profit MoM %
### Definition
```text
(Current Month Operating Profit − Previous Month Operating Profit)
÷ Previous Month Operating Profit
```
### Business Meaning
Measures month-over-month operating profitability growth.

---
## Orders MoM %
### Definition
```text
(Current Month Orders − Previous Month Orders)
÷ Previous Month Orders
```
### Business Meaning
Measures month-over-month order growth.

---
## Gross Revenue QoQ %
### Definition
```text
(Current Quarter Revenue − Previous Quarter Revenue)
÷ Previous Quarter Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures quarter-over-quarter revenue growth.

---
## Gross Revenue YoY %
### Definition
```text
(Current Year Revenue − Prior Year Revenue)
÷ Prior Year Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures annual revenue growth compared to the same period in the previous year.

---
## Refund Amount YoY %
### Definition
```text
(Current Year Refund Amount − Prior Year Refund Amount)
÷ Prior Year Refund Amount
```
### Business Meaning
Measures annual change in customer refunds.

---
## Returned Units YoY %
### Definition
```text
(Current Year Returned Units − Prior Year Returned Units)
÷ Prior Year Returned Units
```
### Business Meaning
Measures annual change in product return volume.

---
## Revenue Growth FY2025 vs FY2024 %
### Definition
```text
(FY2025 Revenue − FY2024 Revenue)
÷ FY2024 Revenue
```
### Business Meaning
Measures overall business growth between FY2024 and FY2025.

---
# KPI Categories
The KPI framework is organized into the following analytical areas:
- Revenue & Sales
- Profitability & Financial Performance
- Returns Analysis
- Product Performance
- Marketing Performance
- Time Intelligence
- Executive Scorecards
- Strategic Planning & Growth Analysis
